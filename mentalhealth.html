Mental Health Resources

Taking care of your mental health is crucial! Here are some facts and resources to help you understand mental well-being better.

Learn About Mental Health

What is Mental Health?

Mental health includes our emotional, psychological, and social well-being. It affects how we think, feel, and act.

Why is it Important?

Mental health is important because it affects every part of our lives, including relationships, work, and overall happiness.

How Can We Improve It?

Engage in activities that reduce stress, get enough sleep, and talk to someone when you're feeling low.
