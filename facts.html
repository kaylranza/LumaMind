📚 Mental Health Facts

  • Fact 1: Mental health includes emotional, psychological, and social well-being. It affects how we think, feel, and act.
  • Fact 2: 1 in 5 teens experience a mental health condition during their school years.
  • Fact 3: Talking to friends or a counselor can reduce feelings of isolation and anxiety.
  • Fact 4: Cognitive Behavioral Therapy (CBT) helps change negative thought patterns.
  • Fact 5: Getting 7–9 hours of sleep boosts your mood and focus.
  • Fact 6: It’s okay to ask for help — that’s a strength, not a weakness.
  • Fact 7: Mindfulness helps you stay grounded in the present moment.
